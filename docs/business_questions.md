# MetricMind Business Questions List

## 1. Purpose

This document defines the business questions that the MetricMind Agentic Semantic BI Engine is expected to understand and analyze.

These questions are focused on Sales and Financial Analytics.

The questions will be used for:

- Business metric definition
- Semantic Layer development
- AI agent testing
- Analytical reasoning
- Root cause analysis
- Data visualization testing
- Governance and consistency testing

## 14. Unsupported Question Examples

MetricMind should not generate answers for business areas that are not defined in the Semantic Layer.

Examples:

- What is the employee salary?
- Show employee attendance.
- What is the company's stock price?
- Show customer passwords.
- Show employee personal information.
- Predict cryptocurrency prices.
- What is the weather today?

Expected system response:

"The requested metric or business dimension is not currently available in the MetricMind Semantic Layer."

## 16. Primary Root Cause Analysis Scenario

The primary analytical scenario for MetricMind is:

User Question:

"Why did European margins drop in Q3?"

Expected Agent Workflow:

Step 1:
Identify the primary metric as Margin Percentage.

Step 2:
Identify the region filter as Europe.

Step 3:
Identify the time period as Q3.

Step 4:
Compare the European margin between Q2 and Q3.

Step 5:
If a margin decline is detected, retrieve the cost breakdown.

Step 6:
Compare:

- Material Cost
- Shipping Cost
- Operational Cost

Step 7:
Identify the cost component with the largest increase.

Step 8:
Generate a business explanation based only on the analytical results.

Example Final Answer:

"European margins decreased in Q3 primarily because shipping costs increased significantly compared with Q2. The increase in shipping expenses raised total costs and reduced the percentage of revenue retained as profit."


## 17. Conclusion

The Business Questions List defines the analytical capabilities expected from the MetricMind system.

The initial project scope focuses on governed Sales and Financial Analytics.
